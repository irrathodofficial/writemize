# Writemize: The Autonomous Content Team

Writemize is an autonomous content team for businesses. Instead of hiring an SEO writer, a trend researcher, and an editor, a business connects their website once, and five coordinated AI agents (Scout, Radar, Quill, Warden, Pulse) research, write, audit, and publish a fully optimized blog post every single day, on schedule, with zero manual work.

## One-Line Pitch
"Writemize gives every business a full-time content team that never sleeps—research, writing, and quality control, fully autonomous, powered by Codex and GPT-5.6."

---

## The 5-Agent Pipeline
Writemize operates as a coordinated orchestration of five specialized agents:

1. **Scout (Business Context Analyzer):** Learns the business's niche, brand tone, audience, and SEO profile from their website.
2. **Radar (Trend & Competitor Research):** Scans Google Trends and competitor content to generate 5 high-relevance, scored topic ideas daily.
3. **Quill (Blog Writer):** Synthesizes business context and trend data to write a full SEO-optimized blog post, complete with a unique featured image.
4. **Warden (Content Auditor):** A meticulous editor that checks keyword density, meta tags, alt text, and readability. It auto-fixes issues to ensure high quality before publication.
5. **Pulse (Scheduler):** Ensures consistent daily publishing at the business’s preferred time.

## Tech Stack
Built during OpenAI Build Week using:
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Tailwind CSS), JavaScript
* **Backend:** PHP 8.x (Custom API endpoints for agent orchestration).
* **AI Engine:** GPT-5.6 (Reasoning & Content) + DALL-E (Featured Images)
* **Database:** MySQL (Relational storage for business profiles, topics, and blog drafts).
* **Orchestration:** Custom agentic pipeline powered by OpenAI Codex

---

##  Why This Wins
*   **Technical Implementation:** Real orchestration of 5 chained AI agents, not just a single prompt.
*   **Self-Correcting Loop:** Warden re-checks and fixes Quill's work, ensuring quality.
*   **Set-and-Forget UX:** Once connected, the content team runs autonomously.
